WHAT IS F1 IN SCHOOLS

F1 in Schools is a program that teaches students about engineering, design and technology through building and racing miniature Formula One cars. It aims to inspire and educate students, encouraging teamwork and problem-solving skills, and raises awareness of the sport and technology behind Formula One. It gives students hands-on experience of applying physics, engineering and technology concepts.

WHO WE ARE

Introducing Cosmic Racing, an elite F1 in Schools team from Larnaca, Cyprus comprising of accomplished students who are fervent about STEM. After 4 consecutive years of participation the team is representing Cyprus at the World finals in Singapore in September 2023.

ABOUT OUR CAR

The car above is the one we will use in the 2023 F1 in Schools World Finals. It has special features that make it a strong competitor. For example, the rear wing is asymmetrical, which helps reduce air resistance and keeps the weight low. The choice of materials also helps keep the weight in the right spot, which helps the car go faster. We have put a lot of research and development into designing this car, so every part has been optimized for speed.
